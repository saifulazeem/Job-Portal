ABOUT US

Who we are

The Tab is the voice of students and the UK’s largest and most influential student media brand. We have three million monthly unique users in the UK - the largest student audience in the country.

Why

We know that trying to find a decent internship or graduate scheme is long. It can take hours of scrolling through mind-numbingly dull job posts to find something that looks even mildly interesting (and doesn’t require 3+ years of experience for an entry-level role).Don’t you want that time back? To give yourself an extra hour lie-in? To binge-watch Euphoria again? To actually have the time to apply for these jobs??!?

What

Specialisation:
Cut the BS and get specialised job alerts based on your career preferences
Quality:
Get access to the best student and graduate-focused job offers and employers
Effort:
Let the companies contact you too, this job hunt doesn’t need to be one-sided
Cheatsheet:
Tips and tricks on our blog will help you ace your applications and interviews

How

Sign up by scrolling down to the form below.

For the sake of inputting one measly email address and clicking a few boxes, you could cut the boredom down to half (don’t quote us on that).
