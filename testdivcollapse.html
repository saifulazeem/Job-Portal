Job Description
Position Title: Mid-Senior Software Quality Assurance Engineer (3+ years industry exp)
Responsibilities:
  • Provide comprehensive and well-structured test plans and test cases
  • Develop standards and procedures to determine product quality and release readiness
  • Develop standards and procedures to determine product quality and release readiness
  • Research and analyse product features being tested
  • Estimate, prioritize, plan and coordinate testing activities for new products/features
  • Research new tools, technologies, and testing processes
  • Discover, identify, isolate, track bugs within software throughout testing, and streamline the whole process by identifying any potential problems
Experience:
  • Experienced in developing QA standards and procedures to determine software product/application/feature quality and release readiness
  • Strong understanding of software functional/non-functional testing i.e. Regression, Load, White/Black-Box, Smoke, Integration, Performance, security testing etc.
  • Proven record of testing WebApi’s using Postman scripting
  • Experienced in testing tools, postman scripting and integration of test results to CI/CD pipelines
Nice to Have:
  • Experienced in test automation tools i.e. selenium/JMeter etc.
  • Proven record of integrating test results to CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins
  • Experienced with open-source test-framework/tools
