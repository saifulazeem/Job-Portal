Accountancy

Accountancy is the measurement, processing and communication of financial information for businesses and corporations.Accountants can work in any industry – whether it’s in an accountancy firm, a financial institution, a professional services firm or even within a startup, accountants are essential to ensure any business is successful. Accountants not only have to be excellent with numbers but also creative – if this sounds interesting, this may be the career path for you.

Forensic Accounting

Forensic accountants lead detailed investigation and data analysis into things that have gone wrong: preventing, uncovering
and solving fraud, bribery, corruption, regulatory breaches, and accounting misstatement. This career will suit those who have
exceptional numeracy skills, attention to detail and a keen investigative streak.

NECESSARY SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS
Skills:
  • Be proficient in databases and Microsoft Office packages
  • Have excellent attention to detail
  • Be adaptable and have a great memory
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent negotiation skills, especially to solve disputes
  • Be trustworthy, and have the ability to understand the meaning of discretion
  • Have the ability to be impartial to events occurring outside of your control
  • Be able to flourish in a high-pressure environment, with excellent time management
